# Gild the Tulip

<div class="author-badge">
  <img src="https://github.com/KaiStarkk.png?size=64" alt="Kieran Hannigan" />
  <a href="https://github.com/KaiStarkk">Kieran Hannigan</a>
</div>

*Gild the Tulip* is the sequel to *Buy the Tulip*: if the first paper lets
CAPM walk us into the most famous bubble in finance, this one asks what happens
when portfolio construction takes fat tails and path dependence seriously. The
answer is less romantic than the title and more useful than the usual efficient
frontier doodle: compounding is lived in sequence, and a strategy that looks
suboptimal on average can be saner for the investor who only gets one timeline.
